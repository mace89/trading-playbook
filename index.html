Play 1

Sweep and Reap

Asia Sweep Model — discretionary setup with mechanical risk management.

Session Rules

  • London Session
  • 3:00 – 5:30 NY time

Core Setup

  • Asia High / Low sweep
  • Clear intent after the sweep

Entry Model

  • IFVG or CISD confirmation
  • No guessing

Risk Management

2R

  • Target 2R only
  • Never extend target

Trade Management

  • Move BE after +1.5R
  • No early BE

Hard Rules

  • No sweep = no trade
  • No confirmation = no entry

Play 2

Matrix

SMT Model — discretionary setup with mechanical risk management.

Session Rules

  • 08:00 – 11:00 NY time
  • Best around 09:30

Core Setup

  • SMT divergence between NQ and ES
  • At highs / lows

Execution Rule

  • Short: execute on asset making lower high
  • Long: execute on asset making higher low

Risk Management

1.5R

  • Stop at SMT high / low

Trade Management

  • Move stop to BE after +1R
  • Exit on counter SMT signal

Hard Rules

  • No divergence = no trade
  • Avoid FOMC / NFP
