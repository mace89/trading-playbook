Play 1

Sweep and Reap

Asia Sweep Model

A discretionary setup with mechanical risk management. Designed to capture momentum after liquidity is taken during London.

Back to top

Session Rules

  • London Session
  • 3:00 – 5:30 NY time

Core Setup

  • Asia High / Low sweep
  • Must sweep Asia High or Asia Low
  • Clear intent after the sweep

Entry Model

  • Wait for one of the following confirmation signals
  • IFVG (Imbalance)
  • CISD (Displacement)
  • Entry only after confirmation — no guessing

Context Rule

  • Best setups occur when London opens near Asia High or Low
  • Then immediately sweeps liquidity
  • If price is far from Asia range, setup quality is lower

Risk Management

2R
  • Target: 2R only
  • Never extend target
  • Never get greedy

Trade Management

  • Move stop to BE only after +1.5R
  • No early BE (avoid getting wicked out)

Markets

  • ES
  • NQ

Hard Rules

  • No sweep = no trade
  • No confirmation = no entry
  • No rule bending
  • Don’t chase extended moves
  • Stick to the plan

Setup Checklist

✓London session active
✓Asia High/Low swept
✓Clean reaction after sweep
✓IFVG or CISD confirmation
✓Entry aligns with direction
✓2R target set
✓Risk defined

Play 2

Matrix

SMT Model

A discretionary setup with mechanical risk management built around SMT divergence and strong/weak asset execution.

Back to top

Session Rules

  • Trade window
  • 08:00 – 11:00 NY time
  • Best setups around 09:30 open

Core Setup

  • Identify relative strength
  • A divergence at highs / lows between NQ and ES

Entry Model

  • Wait for CISD confirmation after SMT
  • Should happen within roughly 5 candles
  • If it drags, the setup weakens

Execution Rule

  • Execute on the stronger / weaker asset
  • Short setup: execute on the asset that makes a lower high
  • Long setup: execute on the asset that makes a higher low

Trade Management

  • Move stop to BE only after +1R
  • Exit on counter SMT signal

Risk Management

1.5R
  • Stop loss at SMT high / low
  • Target: 1.5R only

Context Rule

  • Don’t force entries, take clear setups
  • Take as a top / bottom entry or first pullback
  • Not as a 2nd or 3rd pullback
  • Avoid very high impact news, FOMC, NFP etc.

Markets

  • ES
  • NQ

Setup Checklist

✓SMT divergence present
✓At key high/low
✓CISD confirms within ~5 bars
✓Entry is early (not extended)
✓Not near major news
✓Stop at SMT level
✓1.5R target set
✓Go BE after +1R
